About NSTP Immersion

What is NSTP Immersion?
NSTP (National Service Training Program) Immersion is a program designed to immerse students into different communities to expose them to social realities and promote civic consciousness through actual community service and engagement.

What is its purpose?
The purpose of NSTP Immersion is to develop the studentsâ€™ appreciation of their rights and duties as citizens, encourage active participation in community development, and to enhance their sense of nationalism and social responsibility.

What did it contribute?
NSTP Immersion contributes by empowering youth to become active and committed citizens, building stronger communities through service projects, and fostering collaboration between educational institutions and local communities for sustainable development.
