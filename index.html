About NSTP Immersion

What is NSTP Immersion?
Pamilya Mission: Clean Possible was a community donation drive that aimed to give important everyday items to local residents.
The program encouraged everyone to work together by promoting kindness, cleanliness, and care in the community.

What is its purpose?
The donation drive was held on April 28, 2025, at Exodus Elementary School in Floodway, Taytay, starting at 8:00 AM.
It was a meaningful morning where volunteers and community members came together to share, learn, and connect.

What did it contribute?
We are students from STI College Ortigas-Cainta taking part in the National Service Training Program (NSTP). As part of this program, we want to help communities through service, outreach,and activities like this donation drive.
